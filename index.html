    Things to Know: Mexico City & More
Is Mexico City Safe? Take and wear things you would take and wear as a tourist anywhere else in the world. Plan accordingly. You are more/ just as likely to be murdered in Philadelphia, Las Vegas, Dallas, San Antonio, Miami and LA, than you are Mexico City. Take the same level of consideration you would when travelling to these places.
